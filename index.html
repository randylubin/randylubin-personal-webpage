RANDY LUBIN
CONSULTING ENTREPRENEUR

I'm active in the SF tech scene - advising, mentoring, and consulting with startups. Recently, I've given lectures on entrepreneurship here in California and at academies in India and Malaysia. I'm also exploring my own startup ideas, coding on side projects, and designing board games.

Over the last few years, I earned an MBA at Stanford and was COO of Meetings.io, negotiating its sale to Jive Software. I spent a year at Jive doing product management and corporate development before returning to the startup world.

I grew up in Livingston, NJ and went to Washington University in St. Louis for undergrad. I graduated from Stanford's MBA program in 2011.
