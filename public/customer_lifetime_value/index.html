More info on Customer Lifetime Value ▼

The customer lifetime value (LTV) is the total profit a customer will contribute over his or her lifetime. Net LTV includes the user acquisition cost. A negative Net LTV suggests the business model is not sustainable.

There are several ways to calculate LTV; this calculator keeps things simple. If there's interest, I'll build a more complex calculator which includes virality and net present value. To learn more about Customer Lifetime Value, check out these articles by Fred Wilson and Avinash Kaushik.

Thanks go to Startup Foundry's Startup Death Clock for design inspiration and to Professor Haim Mendelson, who first introduced me to the topic.
